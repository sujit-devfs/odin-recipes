Ingredients
  • Maggi Noodles
  • Onion and Tomato
  • Mix Vegetables
  • Ginger and garlic
  • Butter or oil
  • Fresh coriander
Steps
  1. Heat butter or oil in a pan. Add ginger-garlic paste, onion, and saut√© till the onions are soft.
  2. Next, add tomatoes, mix vegetables, and stir-fry on high flame till they are 90% cooked, and they still have slight crispiness left.
  3. Then add water, maggi masala, red chili powder, garam masala powder, and mix well.
  4. Bring water to boil, add Maggi noodles, close the lid, and cook for 2-3 minutes or until water is absorbed. Stir in between.
  5. Finally, sprinkle chaat masala and switch off the flame.
